About FeedForward

About Us

FeedForward is a smart food redistribution platform connecting food donors, NGO admins, and delivery agents — saving meals, saving lives.

📌 Some Information
🌍
Our Mission
Zero food waste, zero hunger. Every surplus meal deserves to reach a plate — not a landfill. FeedForward makes that connection instant and transparent.
🤝
Community First
Donors earn reward points, NGOs get real-time updates, and delivery agents navigate with live GPS — every role benefits from the platform.
🔒
Transparent & Secure
Every donation is tracked from post to plate. Email notifications, live status updates, and a 6-step tracker keep every stakeholder informed.
📱
Works on Any Device
Fully responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop. Donors post from their phone, agents navigate on the go, admins manage from their desk.
